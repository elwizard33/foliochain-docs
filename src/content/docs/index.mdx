## What is FolioChain Protocol?

FolioChain Protocol (FCP) is a groundbreaking blockchain protocol designed specifically for the tokenization of Costa Rican real-world assets (RWAs). Built with compliance-first architecture and AI-powered verification, FCP bridges the gap between traditional property ownership and decentralized finance.

## Key Features

- **Maritime Zone Compliance**: Automatic enforcement of foreign ownership caps in Costa Rica's Maritime Terrestrial Zone
- **Folio Real Integration**: Direct mapping to Costa Rica's National Registry system
- **Modular Architecture**: Upgradeable smart contracts ready for evolving regulations like Bill 23.415
- **Multi-Asset Support**: From beachfront concessions to coffee farms and conservation land
- **Yield Distribution**: Automated rental income and harvest dividend distribution with tax compliance
